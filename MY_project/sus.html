Suspended Ceilings

Suspended ceilings are a great way to update your space. We install them by hanging a grid from the roof, using strong materials that will last. Our team at Abel Ceiling will work with you to design a ceiling that fits your unique needs and style.
BS Ceiling designs and installs suspended ceilings tailored to your business needs. We use strong frameworks made from exposed tee systems, hanger straps, and shadowline wall angles, with boards in sizes like 1200×600 or 600×600. We offer various lay-in tiles, including acoustic boards for sound absorption, white vinyl for versatility, and other options like fissured vinyl, minerval, and ecophon. Our ceilings enhance acoustics, aesthetics, and efficiency. Serving Sandton and areas around Gauteng, we focus on quality, durability, and customer satisfaction. Contact us today for expert installations that transform your workspace.
