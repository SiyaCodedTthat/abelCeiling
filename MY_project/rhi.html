Rhino-lite Ceiling


Get beautiful and versatile Rhinolite ceilings installed by experts,Rhinolite ceilings are popular because they: Look seamless and smooth,Fit any building style Are perfect for homes, offices, schools, and hospitals Our installations make any space look great
BS Ceilings installs Rhinolite ceilings using top-quality materials, including 9mm or 12mm rhinoboards, moisture-resistant, and fire-resistant boards. These are mounted on a strong T38 grid system, with options for steel or saligna branderings for added strength and style. You can customize your ceiling with finishes like Shadowline plaster-trim and moulded polystyrene cornices to match your interior design. Whether for homes, offices, schools, or healthcare spaces, our skilled team ensures quality work and great results.
