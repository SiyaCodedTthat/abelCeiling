ISO BOARD CEILING


Isoboard Ceilings are renowned for their versatility and practical benefits, offering effective thermal insulation and waterproof properties. This robust ceiling solution enhances both functionality and aesthetics, available in three distinct finishes: pine, bevelled, and flush-joint.
lients have the flexibility to choose from different installation methods based on their needs, whether it’s wooden branderings (32x32mm saligna), steel branderings, or the T38 grid system. Cornices are also available to provide a polished and seamless finish around the edges, ensuring a complete and attractive installation. BS Ceilings ensures professional installation and superior service, catering to residential and commercial clients in Roodepoort and beyond, within the Gauteng region. For those seeking a durable, visually appealing ceiling solution with excellent thermal performance, Isoboard Ceilings from BS Ceilings are the ideal choice.
