Exposed Truss Ceiling


This type of ceiling makes rooms look modern and spacious by showing off the ceilings structure.It creates a sense of openness and makes rooms feel bigger.

These ceilings can be paired with 75mm cove cornices or moulded cornices for a polished look. Installations use either wooden saligna or steel branderings, ensuring both style and stability.
