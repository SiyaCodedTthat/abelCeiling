ISO BOARD CEILING

Isoboard Ceilings are renowned for their versatility and practical benefits, offering effective thermal insulation and waterproof properties. This robust ceiling solution enhances both functionality and aesthetics, available in three distinct finishes: pine, bevelled, and flush-joint.


Bulkhead Installation

Bulkheads are integral parts of ceilings that either protrude from or create recesses within the ceiling, serving both functional and aesthetic purposes. They can be designed to enhance the architectural appeal of a space or to conceal structural elements that might otherwise detract from the overall design
