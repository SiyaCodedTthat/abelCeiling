Isoboard Ceilings are renowned for their versatility and practical benefits, offering effective thermal insulation and waterproof properties. This robust ceiling solution enhances both functionality and aesthetics, available in three distinct finishes: pine, bevelled, and flush-joint.
BS Ceilings creates custom bulkheads to meet your needs. We use the flexible T38 system and offer cladding options like 9mm boards, 12mm boards, or moisture-resistant 12mm boards, ensuring the perfect fit for your space. Whether adding a modern design feature or hiding structural components, our skilled team delivers precise and high-quality finishes.
